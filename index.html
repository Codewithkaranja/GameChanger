About Hon. Isaac Kiprono Rotich

Biography

Hon. Isaac Kiprono Rotich is a dedicated public servant with a proven track record of community development and youth empowerment. Born and raised in Lang'ata, he understands the unique challenges and opportunities facing our constituency.

With a background in community organizing and leadership development, Isaac has consistently demonstrated his commitment to creating positive change through innovative solutions and collaborative governance.

Vision & Agenda for Lang'ata 2027

Youth Empowerment

Skills training, entrepreneurship programs, and tech opportunities for Lang'ata youth to unlock their potential and drive economic growth.

Education

Scholarships, improved school infrastructure, and digital learning initiatives to ensure quality education for all children in Lang'ata.

Healthcare

Accessible community health programs, upgraded clinics, and preventive care initiatives for healthier Lang'ata residents.

Infrastructure

Better roads, efficient waste management systems, and sustainable housing reforms to improve living standards.

Environment

Green Lang'ata initiative with tree planting, clean energy adoption, and environmental conservation programs.

Women & Inclusion

Economic empowerment programs for women and PWDs, ensuring equal opportunities and inclusive development.
